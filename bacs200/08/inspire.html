What does it mean to be an inspiration to others? For some it means someone that provokes others to want to do better in a particular area of their life. For others, it's someone has the ability and willingness to be selfless, creative, innovative, or courageous. When people think of heroes who contribute to society, a rapper wouldn't be the first thing that comes to mind due to the bad images society has painted of them in the past.

The person that has inspired has these characteristics.

  • Outrageously courageous - they disturb the status quo and define a new normal
  • Motivate others - people follow a great leader
  • Big footprint - impact on millions and change the world
  • Sacrifice & commitment - their impact usually costs them everything
  • Integrity - they live true to their calling

Jermaine Lamaar Cole better known as "J. Cole" possesses the heroic quality of courage to face the problems of our society that people choose not to care about. Cole's countless music videos and songs alone, always seem to carry a message and try to reason with the listeners. Cole never had a lot when he was young, so now he does so much to give back to kids all around the country with the "Dreamville Foundation."
